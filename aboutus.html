About
AgroTech

Our Story

As final year students who were inspired to think of a problem in the society and provide a solution, we came together and brainstormed until we came up with the idea of AgroTech. We took a deep look into the agricultural sector and realised most farmers are not acquainted withtechnology. Many farmers have not implemented technology into their farming processes, making their practices more tedious and energy consuming. We researched on IoT sensors and how they can be infused into agriculture. We came to the conclusion that we would provide IoT sensors (soil tempersture sensors and humidity sensors for a start) to farmers for them to easily data via out website platform. A dashboard where farmers can easily view the data from the sensors and provide insights and tips. The farmers would be able to make informed decisions from the data displayed on the dashboard.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a smarter and more sustainable future for farming. We believe that technology, combined with farmers' expertise, can transform agriculture, ensuring food security and prosperity for generations to come. We're committed to providing innovative solutions that empower farmers to thrive in an ever-changing world. To bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and the potential of modern technology. We're dedicated to developing and deploying cutting-edge IoT solutions that provide farmers with the real-time data and actionable insights they need to make informed decisions, optimize resource utilization, and achieve sustainable growth. We believe that by putting the power of data in the hands of farmers, we can contribute to a more resilient, productive, and environmentally responsible agricultural future.

Our Values

Our Services

At AgroTech, we empower farmers with the data they need to optimize their operations through our comprehensive smart farming solutions. We provide:


IoT Sensor Deployment


We strategically place durable, weather-resistant sensors throughout your farm to collect critical data points, including key data points e.g., soil moisture, soil temperature, humidity,etc.

Real-Time Data Monitoring


Our user-friendly platform, accessible via your web browser, displays real-time data visualizations and insights, enabling you to monitor conditions anytime, anywhere.

Actionable Insights


We translate raw sensor data into practical recommendations, helping you make informed decisions about irrigation, fertilization, pest control, and other essential farming practices.


Customized Solutions


We understand that every farm is unique. We work closely with you to assess your specific needs and develop a tailored sensor deployment strategy that maximizes the value of the data.

Intuitive Data Platform


Our powerful yet easy-to-use platform provides real-time access to your farm's data. Visualizations, alerts, and reports make it simple to understand trends and identify areas for improvement.

Precision Sensor Technology


We utilize cutting-edge IoT sensors specifically designed for the rigors of agricultural environments. These sensors are tested for accuracy and durability, ensuring reliable data collection.
